Welcome to the N-Sokoo

Where Sellers meet buyers

The N-Sokoo portal hosts a platform where users can advertise for their items, property, jobs and tenders across East Africa. It has more than 1.5 million visitors monthly.

N-Soko Deals, which will also be available as a mobile app, allows the customer to get a range of products online at a discounted rate. For businesses, it provides an additional revenue stream as an advertising platform.

Other than the N-Sokoo Deals platform, there already exists websites offering deals such as the Kenyan rupu (rupu.co.ke), zetu deals (zetu.co.ke) and Jumia (Jumia.co.ke)
